About Dashboard Manager Add-on

Dashboard Manager is a Google Sheets add-on designed to simplify sports betting record-keeping for the Pro Bettors Academy community. It automates monthly statistics and log management so users can focus on analysis rather than manual sheet setup.

Google Data Usage

This add-on only accesses your current Google Sheet to manage sheets and menus. It requires minimal OAuth permissions: currentonly (access your current sheet) and UI (add custom menus and dialogs). No data leaves your Google account, and nothing is shared externally.
