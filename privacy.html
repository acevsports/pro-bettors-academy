Privacy Policy – Dashboard Manager Add-on

Presented by Pro Bettors Academy

Last updated: November 21, 2025

Introduction

Dashboard Manager is a container-bound Google Sheets add-on built exclusively for the Pro Bettors Academy community. We are fully committed to your privacy and use only the narrowest Google-approved scopes.

Google OAuth Data Access – Limited to a Single Spreadsheet

The Add-on collects no personal data whatsoever. We request only the following two Google OAuth scopes — both are the most limited scopes Google allows for this type of add-on:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets.currentonly
    → Google’s official description: “View and manage spreadsheets that this application has been installed in”
    This scope, combined with the @OnlyCurrentDoc restriction, guarantees the add-on can only read or modify the exact spreadsheet you have open. It cannot access any other file in your Google Drive — even if we wanted it to.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.container.ui
    Used only to display a custom menu and simple alerts inside the open spreadsheet. No data collection occurs through the UI.

No broader scopes are requested. The previous full spreadsheets scope has been permanently replaced with the limited .currentonly version.

How Your Data Is Used

All operations occur entirely within the open spreadsheet using Google’s built-in SpreadsheetApp service. The add-on performs sheet creation, column adjustments, hiding/showing tabs, and formula updates — nothing more.

The Add-on never:

  • sends any data to external servers
  • stores or logs spreadsheet content
  • accesses any other file in your Google Drive
  • shares data with third parties

Data Storage, Transfer & Retention

Zero data is stored or transmitted by the add-on. All processing happens in-memory during execution and is discarded immediately afterward.

Third-Party Access

No third party — including Pro Bettors Academy — ever receives or sees your spreadsheet content. The add-on runs entirely within Google’s secure environment.

Security

All communication uses encrypted HTTPS. Your data remains protected by Google’s infrastructure at all times.

Your Choices & Control

You can revoke access instantly via My Account → Apps with access to your account or by removing the add-on from Google Sheets.

Compliance

This add-on fully complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.
