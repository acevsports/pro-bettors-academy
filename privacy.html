Privacy Policy for Sports Betting Dashboard Google Add-on

Presented by Pro Bettors Academy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

Introduction

Sports Betting Dashboard (“the Add-on”, “we”, “us”, “our”) is a Google Sheets extension designed to optimize, organize, and automate betting dashboard workflows for community users. We are committed to ensuring your privacy and protecting your personal data while using our tools.

Data Collection and Usage

No personal data or sensitive information is collected by the Add-on itself. The Add-on requests only the minimum, essential Google OAuth scopes required for its operation:

  • Access to your current Google Sheet (“currentonly” scope)
  • Permission to add custom menus and dialogs (“UI” scope)

All data processed by the Add-on remains within your Google account and is never transmitted, stored, or shared with any external server.

Use of Community Information

User data, sheet content, and betting dashboard information are solely managed within each user’s Google environment. No add-on features require or enable export, backup, or sharing of your betting data outside Google Sheets.

Aggregated performance analytics or betting dashboard summaries are only available to you within your current sheet.

Third Parties

The Add-on does not connect to, integrate with, or share information with any third-party apps, external APIs, or external databases. You control access permissions and can revoke the Add-on’s access at any time via your Google account settings.

Security

All information processed by the Add-on resides within Google’s secure cloud infrastructure. The Add-on does not store, track, or log any of your data, nor does it retain any information after use.

Your Choices & Control

You may uninstall or disable the Add-on at any time. You may review, limit, or revoke its permissions via your Google account. We do not send marketing communications or advertising of any kind.

Updates & Contact

This policy may be updated to comply with Google’s requirements or to clarify practices. All updates will be announced via this document link.
