Privacy Policy for Dashboard Manager Add-on

Presented by Pro Bettors Academy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

Introduction

Dashboard Manager (“the Add-on”, “we”, “us”, “our”) is a Google Sheets extension designed to optimize, organize, and automate betting dashboard workflows for community users. We are committed to ensuring your privacy and protecting your personal data while using our tools.

Data Collection and Usage

The Add-on collects no personal or sensitive data. It only requests the minimum Google OAuth permissions necessary to operate:

  • currentonly: Access your current Google Sheet to manage sheets.
  • UI: Add custom menus and dialogs to your Google Sheet.

All processing happens within your Google account. No data is transmitted, stored, or shared with any external servers.

Use of Community Information

All data, sheet content, and betting dashboard information remain within each user’s Google Sheets. No add-on features export, backup, or share your betting data externally. Aggregated performance analytics or summaries are only visible to the user within their current sheet.

Third Parties

The Add-on does not connect to or share information with any third-party apps, APIs, or databases. Users retain full control over permissions and can revoke access at any time in Google account settings.

Security

All data remains in Google’s secure cloud infrastructure. The Add-on does not log, track, or retain any information after use.

Your Choices & Control

You may uninstall or disable the Add-on at any time. We do not send marketing communications or advertising of any kind.

Updates & Contact

This policy may be updated to comply with Google’s requirements or to clarify practices. All updates will be posted at this page.
