Privacy Policy for Dashboard Manager Add-on

Presented by Pro Bettors Academy

Last updated: November 9, 2025

Introduction

Dashboard Manager (“the Add-on”, “we”, “us”, “our”) is a Google Sheets extension designed to organize, automate, and streamline betting dashboard workflows. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring full transparency in how we access and use Google user data.

Google OAuth Data Access

The Add-on collects no personal or sensitive user data. We only request the minimum Google OAuth scopes required for core functionality:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets.currentonly
    Used solely to view and modify sheets inside the user’s currently open Google Spreadsheet. The Add-on cannot access or open any other files in your Google Drive.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.container.ui
    Used only to add custom menu items and display dialogs within the Google Sheets interface. No user data is collected through these UI components.

The Add-on’s access is strictly limited to the spreadsheet you have open when running the Add-on. No background access, continuous monitoring, or automated data retrieval occurs.

How Data Is Used

All data processing happens entirely within your Google account. The Add-on performs sheet organization, sheet creation, and dashboard management using only the content of the active spreadsheet.

The Add-on does not:

  • send Google user data to any server
  • store Google user data outside of Google Sheets
  • log, track, or retain any content from your spreadsheet
  • share your data with third parties

Data Storage, Transfer, and Retention

The Add-on stores no data on Pro Bettors Academy servers or any external systems. All operations occur in-memory during execution and are not retained afterward.

No user data, metadata, tokens, logs, or spreadsheet content is transmitted, saved, or persisted by the Add-on.

Third-Party Access

The Add-on does not transmit any information to external APIs, databases, services, or third-party applications. No third party has access to your Google user data through the Add-on.

Security

All data accessed by the Add-on remains protected within Google’s secure cloud infrastructure. Communication between the Add-on and Google services occurs exclusively over encrypted connections (HTTPS).

Your Choices & Control

You may uninstall or disable the Add-on at any time through the Google Workspace Marketplace or your Google account’s permissions page. Revoking permissions immediately disables all access.

Compliance with Google Policies

This Add-on’s use of Google user data complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use Requirements. We do not use or transfer Google user data for purposes unrelated to core functionality.

Updates & Contact

This Privacy Policy may be updated to reflect product changes or Google policy requirements. Any updates will be posted on this page.
